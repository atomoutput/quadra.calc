How to Use quadra.calc

🎵 BPM Input

Enter your song's tempo directly or use the calculate button. Auto-calculation happens as you type.

👆 Tap Tempo

Tap the button in rhythm to automatically detect BPM. Works with 2-8 taps for accuracy.

⏱️ Delay Times

Click any delay time card to copy the value. Visual feedback confirms the copy action.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl/Cmd + Enter - Calculate delays
  • T - Focus tap button
  • B - Focus BPM input
  • Escape - Close modals
